The currency

What is the Gallino

The Gallino (GAL) is the currency of the Principato del Gallo: a crypto-asset issued on the Polygon network with an initial supply of one billion units and a monetary rule carved into its code: the Treasury may mint new Gallini, but never more than 10% of the supply per year, and every minting is public and motivated, forever, on the blockchain. It transfers and spends between wallets like any cryptocurrency, in seconds, for fractions of a cent in fees. The source code is verified: anyone can read it, inspect every transaction and count every Gallino in circulation.

Anyone who wishes may freely buy or sell Gallini through the public exchange pool, with no registration and no intermediaries, at whatever price the pool expresses at that moment.

It is not an investment, not a security, not a promise: it is the identity currency of a community. It is gifted, collected, spent and exchanged among citizens and friends of the Principato — and anyone who wants to join in.

Monetary Charter of the Principato

Disciplined issuance

The initial issue is 1,000,000,000 GAL (one billion). The Treasury may mint new Gallini to accompany the growth of the Principato, but the contract enforces an unbreakable constitutional ceiling: at most 10% of the supply per monetary year. Every minting requires a motivation, recorded publicly and permanently on the blockchain. Anyone may also burn their own Gallini, reducing the supply.

Reserve and liquidity

The Treasury provides the GAL/USDC pool with a symbolic reserve of regulated stablecoin. This reserve makes the Gallino technically convertible, but it is sized for a community project: even modest trades can move the price significantly.

Treasury transparency

The allocation of the issue is declared: 10% to the exchange pool, freely purchasable by anyone, 60% to the Distribution Fund destined for citizens, 30% to the Treasury Reserve. Every movement of these wallets is public and can be inspected on the blockchain by anyone, at any time — including all future mintings and their motivations.

Distribution

Gallini are distributed free of charge to citizens and friends of the Principato, or acquired independently through the public pool. The Treasury does not sell Gallini directly for euros and does not operate as a currency exchange.

Spirit of the currency

The Gallino exists to celebrate the Principato del Gallo and to prove that a community, however small, can give itself a transparent currency. Its value is above all symbolic; any other value is an accident of the market.

Legal notice — read before purchasing

The Gallino is a community token of the Principato del Gallo, an entity not recognised as a State. It does not constitute an investment, a financial instrument, an e-money token within the meaning of Regulation (EU) 2023/1114 (MiCA), nor legal tender.
